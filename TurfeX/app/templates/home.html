Frequently Asked Questions

How do I find games near me?
Simply enable location services and browse available games in your area. You can filter by sport, skill level, and time preferences to find the perfect match.
Is PickUpMatch free to use?
Yes! Basic features like finding and joining games are completely free. Premium features include advanced analytics, priority booking, and exclusive tournaments.
How does the skill level matching work?
Our algorithm considers your self-reported skill level, game history, and community ratings to ensure balanced matches. You can always adjust your skill level as you improve.
Can I organize my own games?
Absolutely! Create your own match groups, set the rules, choose the venue, and invite players. You have full control over your organized games.
What sports are supported?
We support basketball, soccer/football, tennis, cricket, volleyball, and many more. If your sport isn't listed, you can request it through our feedback system.
